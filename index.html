Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

Is BookLane free to use?

Yes, BookLane is completely free to download and use for listing, buying, and exchanging books. We believe in making education accessible and affordable for everyone.

What kind of books can I sell?

You can sell any kind of books on BookLane, including textbooks, novels, and non-fiction. Just make sure the books are in good condition and comply with our guidelines.

How do I get paid for a sale?

Once your book is sold, you will receive payment through your chosen payment method. We support various payment options to make the process seamless for you.

Is BookLane available on iOS?

Yes, BookLane is available on iOS devices. You can download it from the App Store and enjoy all the features on your iPhone or iPad.
